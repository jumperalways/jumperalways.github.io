Nexus

Features

The nexus can be accessed by sleeping in the large bedroom.

This is a dark area featuring 4 altars and two warp points. There are trees, server blocks(?) and several one-eyed floating red monsters in this area but cannot be interacted with.

The north altar takes you to Floating Light World, the south one takes you to Paradise World, the east one to Museum and the west one to Spider Abyss. If you have been to the Waterfalls and activated the warp point from there, a new warp point will appear at the southeast corner of the nexus. Similarly if you have visited the Church and come back to the nexus through the warp, a new warp will appear at the northeast corner.
